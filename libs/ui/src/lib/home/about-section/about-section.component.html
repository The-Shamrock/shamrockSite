About the Shamrock

We are The Shamrock - the student run magazine for the Mizzou College of Engineering! The Shamrock first formed in 1906, only three years after the first E-Week. In our current form, we are a digital magazine hosted right here on this website. We seek to inform, entertain, and bring together engineers all over Mizzou. So sign up for our email list and follow us on social media to say in the loop with what's going on in the College and with your fellow students!
